55
"Nar'm, I'se tellin' you de trufe.
Moses, dat nigger huxter, nebber open
his jaw ter say anudder wud ter me
'bout lub den, ner sence. He knowed
dat dem ebil eyes er his'n had done en
done de bizness fer 'im a pow'ful sight
better den any wuds could do. I didn'
hab no min' er my own. 'Deed 'm, I
didn', en so I jes' went ter wuk gittin'
raidy ter be marr'ed-jes' lak he say I
mus' do. I didn' know fer sho', but I
s'posed gittin' marr'ed wuz w'at he wuz
'ferrin' ter, w'en he th'eaten me wid dat
chickin cunjer.
