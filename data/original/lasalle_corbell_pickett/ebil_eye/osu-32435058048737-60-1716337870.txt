EBIL EYE
"Dat's de onlies' wud dat dat green-
gray-eyed nigger ebber said ter me
'bout lub, er 'bout gittin' marr'd, needer
'fo' ner sence. He nebber eben look
back, ner nebber wait fer no answer
boutn w'edder I'd go wid 'im er not.
He jes' went long out, en got in his
kyart en driv' off, en lef de chickins on
de flo' onconshubble, en me kinder
stunted en onconshubble, too, a stan'in'
dar a watchin' en a waitin' ter see ef
needer one er us, me er de chickins,
would come to en git our ret senses
back ag'in any mo'. Den I kinder
mimicked myse'f bein' slung roun' en
spun in de a'r lak de chickins wuz
spun, wid my haid tucked un'neaf my
arms lak dar wings wuz tucked, 'twell
you know, I 'clar' I got kinder addle-
minded.
