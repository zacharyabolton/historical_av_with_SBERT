EBIL EYE
byar de naix day ef I had ter tote yis-
tiddy's tribulashun-burdens 'long wid
dat day's tribulashun-burdens. Nar'm,
I'd pe'ish un'neaf de load er restress ef
I did dat, so I jes' libs one day at de
time.
"W'en I gits thoo wid my yistiddys
I leabes my termorrers ter de bressed
Lawd. Co'se de empreshun er de yis-
tiddy's yoke is 'bleeged to be on yo'
heart somewhar, en leabes hits skyars
en hits smears, fer somehow er 'nudder
a praher-rubber kyan' rub de skyars out.
Skyar-writin' is lak de writin' on de
wall, only dem dat he'ped ter mek de
skyars en de smears kin mek out de
readin'. T'ank Gord, nobody else but
Him en dem dat dipped dar han's in yo'
soul's red skyarlet blood, en sopped in
de same dish wid you, en has stuck dar
