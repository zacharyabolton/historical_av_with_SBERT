EBIL EYE
wid sperits bofe, how dat one match en
dat one li'l' blue light, en dat 'signifer-
can' tin box kin bek my breffus rolls,
br'ile my ste'k, en b'ile my coffee-pot,
en dat, too, in de broad open daytime, is
mo' dan I kin see. Gord knows, hit's
beyonst me.
"Deze yer w'ite folks nowadays is
so curisome, en so fitified, en dey's up
ter sech tricks you kyan' tell nuttin'
'tall much 'bout 'em. Yer I's been a
totin' chips en cinders all my bawn days,
en my mammy en she's mammy, en
granny 'fo' dem, en I hain't had no
'kashun but ter aspeck ter keep on a
totin' 'em 'twel Marse Gabe blows his
ole trumpet, en we po' niggers sheds our
brack skins, en dis yer ban' er Cain, en
all dis yer cullud ackstinkshun 'twix'
