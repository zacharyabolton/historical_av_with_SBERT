"De bull-frogs jes' ez soon ez ebber
dey years de sperit er Ung' Frenigike a
prowlin' 'roun' grunts up dar th'oats
wid grunts en croaks, en halloes out
'grunkety-krung' en 'chunkety-plung'
'twel dey 'sembles deyse'fs tergedder,
en den dey all lips up in de a'r en doves
down un'neaf de water-'kerblink, ker-
blunk! kerblink, kerblunk!'-one atter
de yudder; en den dey all rises up ag'in
wid a luperty-clup, luperty-clup, en jines
in wid one anudder en sings de bull-
frog song er welcome ter de gre't sperit
er Ung 'Frenigike, who, dey says, watches
ober bofe dem en Sandy Bottom-
