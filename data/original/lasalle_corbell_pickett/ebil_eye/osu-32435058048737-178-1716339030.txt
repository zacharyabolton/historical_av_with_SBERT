dat wid de gob'ment.
"A cat hain't gwine to w'ar her-
se'f en her smellers bofe out scratchin'
'roun' en sniffin' atter mices w'en dar's
a nice fat canary walkin' 'roun' loose

side er her. Nar, suh, ner me, needer.
Me en de cat bofe er us is got a heap
too much ph'los'fy roots a growin' on
de insides er our un'erstan'in' fer dat.
I tell you de trufe, Marse Da', nigger-
stummuck don' nebber get tired a eatin'
gob'ment. Dis yer rashun bizness is a
moughty insecatin' bizness."
