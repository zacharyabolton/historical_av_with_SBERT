wid lime, but we didn' git along terged-
der ez well ez we did apart, suh. W'y,
Marse Da', dat ooman wuz kantanker-
ous. Now, jes' look at de onseasonable-
ness er de hour she eben down tuck ter
lay down en die in. She nebber meked
de firs' rassle wid def, suh. Nar, suh,
she jes' tuck en die so easy I didn' know
fer certain wedder she's sho'-nuff daid
er not.
