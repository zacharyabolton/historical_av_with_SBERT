'fo' me stood de brazenes' face-piece er
yaller-flesh you ebber seed.
En you

mistis dar den; dat she wuz Mrs.

b'yt'ing wuz hern, en dat ef I knowed
w'at wuz de bes' fer my good, I'd git
up en git 'fo' dar wuz any trouble.
