Bvt. Brig. Gen'l O. Brown,
Assistant Commissioner, &
Richmond, Va.
Sept. 4 1867.

Washington,

General:

I respectfully forward herewith Transportation Orders, No. 2221, for transportation of Margaret Frazier & others, Freedchildren the same having been requested by letter of Tho's P. Jackson, Agt., forwarded to this office, approved by your endorsement of date August 30th 1867.

By order of Maj.-Gen. O. O. Howard, Commissioner.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

H. M. Whittelsey
Bvt. Brig. Gen., Chief Q. M., Bureau R., F., & A.L.
