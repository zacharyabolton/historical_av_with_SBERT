August 9th 1859

Waynesboro

Dear John

I Send enclosed this morning my Execution against the Two -- Wheelers in all Speed. I hope you may Corner the Boys.

how are you progressing -- against Capt. Hall, do you know anything about a young man named Joseph I. Wolf. He lives or did live near Massie Mill he owes me about $38.

Yours Truly

Wm. W. King

To John H. McCue Esqr.
