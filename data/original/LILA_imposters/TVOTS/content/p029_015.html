Hon Alex H H Stuart Staunton VA

June 16/67

Philada

Dear Sir

Yours of the 13this to hand, am Sorry You had the trouble to copy the agreement, what I wanted was a copy of the paper I left with you in pencil, extracts taken from the Ledger showing the alterations made therein; which I should like to have at your earliest convenience.

I think upon reflection that our best course will be to put him Bankruptcy My council here Mr. Bullett will add a postscript to my letter and I beg you will consult with him in the matter.

Very Truly Yours

P Brady

Please direct your letters to 228 Walnut Street.
Dear Sir
I suggested to Mr. Brady that perhaps you would proceed in Bankruptcy. This is however for you. If you think the the object he has is to remove his property from the reach of his creditors I should think you could do so. I only make the suggestion for your consideration. Yrs truly
John C Bullitt
