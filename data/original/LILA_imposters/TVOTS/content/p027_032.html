April 22, 1867

Augusta Co. Va.

We the undersigned parties to a contract between Samuel Parent of the first part and William Saulsbury of the second part hereby agree to refer all matters in dispute between us to Dr. Samuel Kennedy A. J. Thompson, and mutually bind ourselves to abide by the terms of his decision and to make prompt settlement in accordance therewith.

William X his mark Saulsbury

Witness to signature of William Saulsbury
Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
B.R.F. & A.L.
