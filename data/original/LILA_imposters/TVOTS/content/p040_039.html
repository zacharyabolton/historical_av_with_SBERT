Waldo, Roswell
Clerk
Dec. 21, 1868

Richmond, Va.

Sir,

I am directed by the Asst. Comr. to inform you that by direction of the Secy of War, your appointment as an agent of this Bureau has be revoked, to date December 31st 1868 in consequence of the discontinuance of this Bureau at that time.

Yours respty

Will A. Coulter
Bvt. Capt. U.S. Army
A.A.A.Genl.
