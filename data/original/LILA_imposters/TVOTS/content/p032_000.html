Sept 19/67

Elizabeth Furnace

Dr Sir

Yours of 16th at hand in refference to Sam Calaways act with me, [unclear: we] claim to owe him nothing, I will not now attempt to explain the case, but will be in town in a few days & will see you.

Yours very respectfully

R B Dunlop
for D.V.H. [unclear: Fosser]
