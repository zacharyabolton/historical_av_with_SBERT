Alex H. H. Stuart Esq

Jan 17/68

Philada

Dear Sir

I heard this Morning from a friend in New York that Danl Brady was Very sick please inform me what you have done in his case and when You expect to get judgment out against him, I trust he has not disposed of the Bath furnace property, I hope You will prevent his disposal of the property, if it can be done, waiting your answer
I remain

Very truly Yours

P. Brady
