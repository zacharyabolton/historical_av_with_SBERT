June 1st, 1868

Staunton, Va.,

We, Jno G. A. Crawford, Edward Baldwin, Archer Brock, and Isaac Carter and E. Stibling as Trustees of the Freedmans School House, known as the Waynesboro School House, located in the town of Waynesboro, Augusta Co., Va. in the 5th Magisterial district, hereby pledge ourselves, our heirs, assigns, and executors in behalf of the Freedmen of said district our solemn guarantees that said school House shall be from this time henceforth and forever, used for school purposes and shall be for such purposes, under the control of our successors &c for the purposes named.

Witness
