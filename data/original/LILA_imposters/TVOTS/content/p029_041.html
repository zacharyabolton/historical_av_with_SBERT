Capt. John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
Winchester

July 2d 1867.

Staunton, Va.,

Captain

Reily McDonald (c) calls frequently to know if the Bill for Bal. $8 50/100 due to him by Mr. C.W. Oltmann, sent from this office April 24th (Lett. No. 60 [unclear: v.1]/67) has been paid, as he needs the money. McDonald is sure that Oltmann is at Lexington.

Yr. obt. servt
Thos P. Jackson
Agent
