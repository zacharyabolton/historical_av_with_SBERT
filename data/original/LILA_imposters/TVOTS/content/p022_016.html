Nov 6th

P. S. Since writing the foregoing Mr Tukey has received yours of the 2d [unclear: inst.] says he had given up hearing expecting to hear from you it was so long since he not A. M. now so far but another teacher will needed here soon and it would be well if it could be a man. I shall unless otherwise instructed return to the Valley after going to Richmond and Norfolk which will be in the course of this week. Write me at the latter place

Coan

Teachers on arriving there will find Mr Tukey living near R. R. Depot in Count House yard (Breckenridge House) Mr Tukey will be on the look out for them if he is in Town
