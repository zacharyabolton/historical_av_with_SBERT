Dear Sir: -

Solon informs me that there are about 30 colored children in his place who [unclear: would] attend a school if they had a teacher - They have a school room already & Every thing ready for the opening of the school. - Will Martha Hanger be able to teach? if so, she can have the - Her board will be furnished her salary which will be agreed upon [unclear: when] [unclear: she] accepts the place. -
