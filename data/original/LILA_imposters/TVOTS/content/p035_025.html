Bt. Brig. Gen O. Brown Asst. Comm. Va

January 27, 1868

Winchester, Va

Respectfully referred to

Gabriel Brown, of this place states that he can support his family, but is now unable to pay their traivlling expenses from Staunton - I therefore respectfully recommend that transpor- tation be provided for Betsy Brown, and three children, aged 10, 3, 2 for Staunton, Va to Winchester
