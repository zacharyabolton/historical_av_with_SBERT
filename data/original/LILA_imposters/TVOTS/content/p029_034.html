Mr. Thos P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton Va

June 25th 1867

Winchester, Va.,

Sir

You will notify Mr William Riley, the Lessor of the building rented by the Bureau for a School for Whites at Staunton, of the termination of the Lease, to take effect at the end of the present month.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Svt

John A. McDonnell
Capt. & Sub Asst Comr
