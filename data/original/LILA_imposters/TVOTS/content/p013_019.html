Mr.H.H. Hamilton

February the 17th/63

Camp New Salem, VA

Dear Brother,

I have been looking for this stamp I wrote to you for this week so I will have to remind you again Hen I'm out entirely all the letters I write I have to endorse & if you don't send them stamp you will have to pay all the postage Well Hen my Registration came back the other day disapproved so I can't get off at present & will have to get the uniform shave bought the cloth at [unclear: 30] dollars per yard for the clothit will last when completed [unclear: 16225] Capt. Cochran was elected Col Gibson Lt Col Eakels Maj. last Thursday of the [unclear: 14th Reg VaCal] Hen I want you to send me at least fifty stamps five dollars worth & call on Maggie for the money if I never get back. Hen I will also send an advertisement in this I wish you would have published in Spectator if you please & charge me with the bill. Well I must go close for the present Give my love to all my caring friends & look over a hurried written letter.

Yours very truly

Jos.A. Wilson, 14th Reg Va Cavalry

A Large Fight
Wanting Five Hundred (500) houng puppies in the 14th Reg Va Cal for the purpose of devouring the Rotten Beef rancid to the men as rations & the said pups to be delivered at Salem Va & Coll. on Maj Neuman for the sum of the prise of the puppies
February the 17th 1863
