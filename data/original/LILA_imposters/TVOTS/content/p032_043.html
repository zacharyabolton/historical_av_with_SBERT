Thos P Jackson Esqr
Staunton Virginia

October 25th 1867

Wilmington Delaware

Respected Sir

I have written to my son at Livingston Ala but the letter has been returned to me unclaimed On Aug 3d you kindly gave me the directions where to write but it seems strange that no such direction found him. I enclose the letter to you and beg you would try and find him for me if not to much trouble.

Yours truly
and respectfully

Isaac Galloway
per WBW
