Jno H McCue Esq
Jany 1/58

Your letter received and I. went to every place I. thought I could procure you a cook or nurse but could find none all having been disposed of. I hear Tho Bowan near Greenwood Tunnel has a good cook for hire if you are not supplied you had best write to him or come over and see him such as your want is hard to find hour respects to you and lady

Your friend Very Truly

W. W. Gibbs
