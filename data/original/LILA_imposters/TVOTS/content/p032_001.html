Mr. Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton Va.

Sept. 19th 1867.

Winchester Va.,

Sir

Instructions have been received to forward all vouchers for purchase in time to reach Richmond, Va. by the 25th of the present month. You are therefore directed, if there are any purchases to be reported as made this month, in completing the schoolhouse at Waynesboro, to forward them immediately.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Svt.

John A. McDonnell
Capt & S.A.C.
