Brig. Genl. O. Brown
Asst. Comr. Dist. of Va.
Richmond (Through HdQrs 9" Sub Dist Va)
Jan 31st 1868.

Staunton, Va.

General

In compliance with Cir. Order No 10 6 BRF & AL S. 1866 I have the honor to report that except examination before magistrates no trials involving the rights of Freedmen have taken place in either county or circuit courts in the 4th Division but several freedmen are in custody awaiting trial at March term.

Examinations before Magistrates have been repeatedly attended by me and found conducted fairly except occasionally excessive bail demanded, which in deserving cases, I have procured an abatement of.

I have the honor to be General
Your obt servt

Thos P. Jackson
Asst Sub Asst Comr
