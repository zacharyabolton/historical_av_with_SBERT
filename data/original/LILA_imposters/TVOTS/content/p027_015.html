Col. G. Malory
A.A.A.G. B.R.F. & A.L.
Richmond Va.
Through Dist. Hd. Qrs.

April 1st 1867

Staunton Va.

Col.

I have the honor to report in compliance with Circular No. 10 C.S. 1866 that no Criminal Trials were brought to my knowledge in this Sub-Dist during the month of March, 1867.

I am Col. with much Respect
Your Obt Servt.

Frederick S. Tukey
Asst. Supt.
