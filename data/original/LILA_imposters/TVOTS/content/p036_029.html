Rev R. M. Manly
Supt. Education
Mch 18, 1868

Staunton, Va.,

Sir

In reply to your letter of March 14th will the lady go to a country village or desire to live at Staunton. I will prosecute enquiry but this information would make it much easier for me to report.

Yr obt servt

Thos P. Jackson
A.S.A.C.
