To the Agent of Freedmens Bureau Staunton Va.

Decr 22nd 1868

Craigsville Augusta Co. Va.

Dear Sir

Please inform me by return mail if all the Registration Lists are in your office -- there is nine of them and I left them in that office at the request of Col. John W. Jordan some months ago and much oblige.

Your Humble Servt

G.W. McCutchen
President Bd Registration
Augusta Co Va
