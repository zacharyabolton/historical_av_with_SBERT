Rev S Hunt

March 23rd 1866

Dear Sir

Will you have the kindness to enclose a note to Dea Bronson or The 1 Cong Church with this letter of mine and after you have read it send them both on to Waterbury as directed.

I want you to state that the letter came through your hands and add a solicitation,-- telling them perhaps that I am work for you--but I will leave you to get up the letter Please send it without much

Very Respectfully

John Scott
