Brevet Brig Genl O. Brown
A.A.A.G.
Richmond Va (thro' HdQrs Seventh Dist. Va)

Oct. 18th 1866

Staunton Augusta Co. Va.

General:

I have the honor to request "Leave of Absence" for five (5) days to visit Washington D.C. on important private business.

I have been on duty in the "Bureau" since the 2d of April 1866 during which time I have not had a "Leave of Absence."

Very Respectfully
Your Obt. Servant

Geo. T. Cook.
1st Lieut. V.R.C. and Asst. Supt.
