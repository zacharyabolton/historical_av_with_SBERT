Oct 30th 1860

Staunton

John H. McCue Esqr
D. Sir

I Suppose you will think I. am tobblesome but it is neddsesaty that makes me write to you a gain I. am hard up and compeld to raise Some money or have my property Sold by the Sheriff is thare annay way by which you can healp me Some you wrote to me that you expected to make Some arrangement to get Some money it wood be a very grate accomodation to me at this time if you could do Something for me

Very Respetfully

Jas J. A. Trotter
