May 4th 1868

Winchester,

Colonel

The Chief Quartermaster having decided that Full Requisitions should be made by each office & agent for the full expended in their offices, you will please signand return enclosed blank forms at your earliest convenience.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt St

John A. McDonnell
