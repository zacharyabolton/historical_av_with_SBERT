Feby 29.68

Winchester, Va.

Sir

Your Receipt Rolls were received, and account for all clothing with which you are charged, except the following articles, viz.-

3 Gt Coats [unclear: Supy] - 1 Shirt, 1 pr Mens shoes & 2 pr Children's shoes.

To avoid making out two sets of Rolls for so small an issue, you will please send the names of the persons to whom these articles are issued, and I will enter them upon the Rolls kept here.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt. Svt.

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
