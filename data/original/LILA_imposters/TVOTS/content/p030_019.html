Thos P. Jackson, Agt.

July 22d 1867

Woodstock, Va

Sir

Please inform the parties (through council) to the suit of the M.E. Church in Staunton, that no evidence will be heard in the case, except that reduced to writing, also, that council can argue the case, if they so desire, the hearing of which has been deferred until July 31st 1867.

Respectfully
Your obt Servt

J.T.H. Hall
Lt V.R.C. A.A.A.G.
& Mil Comr
