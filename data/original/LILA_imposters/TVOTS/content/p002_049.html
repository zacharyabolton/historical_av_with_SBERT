Agreement made this day between W m Walker and D. N. VanLear, witnesseth that said Walker (cold) has this day hired to said VanLear his son Governor to live with and labor for, and be subject entirely to the control of sd VanLear until the 25th of December 1867. In consideration of whose Services the said VanLear agrees to pay to said Wm. Walker or his order the sum of Sixty Dollars and to have the said sons clothes washed.

Witnesseth our hands this the 4th January 1867

Signed Wm his mark X Walker
D. N. VanLear

Test F. D. Dillinger

This is to certify that the above is a true copy of an article in my hands between Wm Walker cold & D. Newton VanLear, May 11th 1867.

S. Paul

Date of agreement between VanLear & Walker Jany 4/67 3 months & 18 days according to Walkers statement.
