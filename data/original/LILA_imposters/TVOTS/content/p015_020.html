Sept 22/63

Staunton Va

Dear Wife

This note will be handed you by Miss M S Hieskell who is on her way to Phil a to take up residence. I will be glad that you will have her remain with you during her stay in the [unclear: our] [unclear: Town] and give her any assistance she may need to help her on her journey. introduce her to Cousin Mary L. Kelley. she will hand you a note from me & Can give you some verbal information from me

Yours ever

J Q A Nadenbousch

Your husband is very well. I have not time to stop and see you. He says if you Can bring your household furniture Come on, but not otherwise I will forward your letter to him at [unclear: my] [unclear: home]. Write only

one page and no war news. Address Miss Mary S. Heiskell 719 Walnut Street Philadelphia. M rs H J Nadenbousch
