Thos. P. Jackson
Agent, Bur. R.F. & A.L. Dist. Va

March 24th 1868.

Richmond, Va.,

Sir,

I enclose herewith your appointment as Agent of this Bureau. The accompanying oath, after being duly executed, will be returned to these Head Quarters.

By order of Bvt. Brig. Genl' O. Brown, U.S.V.,
Asst Comr

Will A. Coulter Bvt. Capt. US Army
A.A.A.Genl'
