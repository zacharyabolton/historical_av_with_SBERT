Mr Jn H McCue

July 14t 59

Staunton

Dear Sir

Your favor of the 13 th Inst. is received, containing two notes for Eighty six dollars & fifty cents each.

We in return enclose the old bond of $160.57 which we hope will come safe to hand.

Accept our thanks for your promptness, and believe us ever to be

Your Sincere Friends

Piper & Funkhouser
