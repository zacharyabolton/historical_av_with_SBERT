June 16 1868

Staunton

My Dear Mollie

I rived home Safely & had quite a plesent ride. Tho I did Not Enjoy it as much as the ride we had up the River in the Evening. I did Enjoy it So much. I did wish you could have come back with me. Ella will moove this week & why cant you come up with me when I came down a gain you & Ella can then go up to the Furnace to gether it would be such a plesent trip for you you could Spend a week or so in the mountains very plesently I would Not want you to Stay too long for I would ragther for you to Spend all your time in Staunton. I think you told me Some time ago that you Entended to Spend the Summer with Ella I hope you will & make up your mind to come back with me Saturday a week if we live wont you? I know your Ma will Not object to your coming

You ask me Several times if I enjoyed my visit Yes and I all ways Enjoy my visits I am all ways happy when with you my love the greatest plesure I have in this world is viseting you at your home never think I am not happy or dont Enjoy my self for I do, & I love you with all the Strenth of my Soul & mind I love you with true devotion of my heart. Magnus & Ella will be down Saturday if they live. Tell your Ma Mr Sheets Sad he would take Miss Maggie's Photographs down with him to day my love to your Ma. love to all

I Send a loveing Kiss to you. I love you with all my heart & Soul may God Bless you. Good bye

Yours

D H Evans
