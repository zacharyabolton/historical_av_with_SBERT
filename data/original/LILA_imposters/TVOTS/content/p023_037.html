Capt. Geo. W. Wells
AQM & Supt
Sub. Dist. E H. Ferry Va.
Feby 14th 1866

Captain

During my temporary absence on a tour through the upper portion of my District, you will please assume the conduct of the military office business here, referring matters of [unclear: special] importance to me at Staunton, Va, up to the 24th of this month.

You have authority to sign official papers for me during such absence.

I am &c

WSH
Capt &c
