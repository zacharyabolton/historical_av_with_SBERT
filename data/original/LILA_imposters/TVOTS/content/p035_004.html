Jay 16th 1868

Sangersville

Sir

I received your note of the eleventh in regard to Richard Banheart's work. I will come up next week and settle with him, if no providential interferance I would have come before now, but his clothes is hear, and I did not want to come untill I could come with the wagon, and bring his clothes.

Yours Respectfully

J.F. Hoffman

To P Jackson
