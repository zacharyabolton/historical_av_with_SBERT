Col. O. Brown
Asst. Comr.
Richmond
Septr 9 1865

Winchester

Colonel

I have the honor to return the enclosed papers with the information that the girls Lucy and Maria are now living with Miss Mary Meade at White Post, Va and respectfully apply for transportation for Lucy and Maria Meade from Winchester Va to Greencastle Pa to which place they will be forwarded upon the receipt of the necessary transportation.

I am, Colonel
Respy yrs

W Storer How
Capt &c
