Bt. Brig. Gen. O. Brown
Asst. Comr.
Richmond, Va
Sept 11th 1867

Winchester, Va.

General

In compliance with Circular Letter from your HeadQuarters dated Septr 3rd 1867: I have the honor to transmit a List of Employees in this District, showing their duties, names, place where employed, born, and places whence appointed, with annual compensation of each.

Very Respectfully
Your Obet Servant

John A. McDonnell
Captain & S.A.C.
