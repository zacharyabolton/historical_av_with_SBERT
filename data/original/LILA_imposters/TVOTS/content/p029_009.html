Mr. Joseph Milsted

June 4, 1867

Augusta Co

Sir

Peyton Harris (c) complains of bad treatment of his child by your wife and that you attempt to break an agreement made with him for present year. I need not tell you that the law gives you no right to beat or threaten anyone and before you can put Harris out of his house & garden you must assign sufficient reason, such as breach of agreement on his part.

Yr Obt Srv

Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
