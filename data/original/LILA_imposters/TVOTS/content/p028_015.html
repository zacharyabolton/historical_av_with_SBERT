Mr. Samuel Paul

May 11/1867

Mt. Solon

Sir

William Walker (c) says you hold a written contract between him and Mr Newton Van Leer for hire of his son Governor. Please send me without delay either original contract or certified copy over your own signature.

Yr obt servt
Thos P Jackson
Agent

Mr S. Paul brings copy of contract to off. BR F & AL May 12/67
