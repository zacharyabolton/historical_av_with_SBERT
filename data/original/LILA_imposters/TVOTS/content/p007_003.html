June 18th 1861

Head Quarters Company K
Winchester

Col Harper
Sir

I have not succeeded in getting the Minnie Rifles yet. I have another plan on foot now It is to have the Enclosed Requisition Signed by Yourself and Col Jackson. Will you be kind Enough to do the best you can for me as I am very anxious to be prepared to do Service Learn from Col Jackson if you please if it is necessary to keep those men of my company who are guarding the Winchester and Potomac Rail Road and [unclear: Opequon] Bridges any Longer on that Service.

Very Respectfully

John Avis
Capt 5th Infantry
