Bvt Brig Gen. O. Brown
Asst Comr &c
Richmond Va

July 11th 1868

Staunton, Va.,

General

I have the honor to request "Leave of Absence" for Ten (10) days from this Post for the purpose of visiting a younger brother in Chicago Ill. who has recently met with a terrible accident and whom I have not seen for years.

In this connection I desire to call your attention to the fact that this is only the second time I have requested leave of absence to go beyond the limits of this District since being assigned to duty in this state, Jany 26 1866

I am General
Very Respectfully
Your obt Svt

Jno. W. Jordan
A.S.A.Comr &c
