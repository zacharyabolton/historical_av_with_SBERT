Mr Jno [unclear: Gear]

Dec 12/68

Staunton Va

Sir:

Jno Randolph (colored) complains that you owe him $4 00 for 12 days labor and that you refuse to pay him.

I have the honor to request your appearance at this office today at 3 o'clk to receive your testimony in regard to the matter & a prompt settlement of the a/c.

Roswell Waldo
