Bt. Brig. Gen. O. Brown
Asst. Comr
Richmond, Va
Apl 22d 1866

Winchester, Va

I have the honor to request that you will forward me transportation from this point to Afton, Albemarle Co. for Louisa Frintz, a freedwoman who is at Staunton, sick and destitute, and dependent upon the Bureau for support; she has no home here, and will be cared for at Afton by her parents who reside there.

I am, General
Very Respectfully
Your Obedt Srvt

W. Storer How
Bt Maj & AQM
Superintendent
