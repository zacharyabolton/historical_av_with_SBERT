Nov 6 1868

Lexington, Va.

Mr Waldo

Your line recd relating to the RR claim & Transportation matter. The first is collected and all right the other I cannot understand and can get no information concerning it but will send the money back and take the [unclear: risk].

As soon as I get my [unclear: assets] in which I am expecting daily will send you amount covering back that is $27.14/00 dollars.

Yours &c

Jno W Jordan
