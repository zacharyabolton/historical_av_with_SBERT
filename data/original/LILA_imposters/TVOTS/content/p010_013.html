April 21, 1862

Dear father

I seet my self to inform you all that I am well and hope those few loines and find you all well. I receve your letter dated march the 26. I was glad to here from you all gave my love to mother + Brothers + sisters you wanted to no when I could come home I do not no when but I dont think it will be very long I must bring my letter to a close buy saying write soon as you get my letter

and remember your son until death

Samuel M Moses

To Charles Moses
