Dec 23 1868

Staunton, Va.,

In compliance with instructions contained in Circular No 32, from Asst Comr Richmond Va dated Dec 19, 1868, I have the honor to report that I have this day turned over "Register of Marriages" for Augusta Co. to Mr. W. A. Burnett County Clerk, whose receipt therefor, I enclose herewith.

No records of Marriages for Highland Co. on file here.

Very respectfully
Your obt servant

Roswell Waldo
A.S.A.Comr
