On the 25 day of December 1864 we or either of us Bind ourselves our heirs & co to pay to Mrs. BetsyPolly [unclear: W] & Robberts Seven Hundred Dollars for the hire of two servant men & one woman names as follows. Woodson , George & Jenna - - - . This year and to furnish them with the usual amounts of clothing except Blankets

Witness our hands & seal this 28th Dday December 1863

N. H. Hotchkiss (Seal)
W B Young (Seal)

If the above Servants are rehired Mrs Robberts is to have the privalege of hiring them or finding them places. N. H. Hotchkiss
