Mr. Thos. P. Jackson
Agent 4th Div.
Staunton, Va.

May 15th 1867

Winchester, Va.,

Sir

By this days mail, I send you a supply of blanks for registry of children, and of Marriages required by Circular No. 11 Series of 1866. HdQrs. Asst. Comr, Va.

You are expected to hasten its completion in your Division. More blanks will be sent if needed.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt Svt

John A. McDonnell
Capt. V.R.C.
Sub Asst. Comr
