Miss Amelia Mead
Staunton, Va.

December 9th '9

Madam,

I enclose herewith for your signature, vouchers for repairs on school-house at Staunton, Va. - amount $17.00

Please sign them and return to this office as soon as possible. Vouchers will be filled at this office.

Yours, respty

R. M. Manly
Supt. of Ed.
