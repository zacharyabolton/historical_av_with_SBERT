Feby 11th 1860

Staunton

Mr. J. Howard McCue
[unclear: Dr] Sir

I enclose Bill for amt due [unclear: us], which I hope you will find It convenient to remit as we (like I suppose everybody else) are much in need of funds, and find It very difficult too meet our engagement and but for pressing necessity, I would not trouble you, as I am satisfied you have not forgotten us,

Hoping to hear from soon and with my best wishes for your Success

I Remain Yours

J. V. Alby
