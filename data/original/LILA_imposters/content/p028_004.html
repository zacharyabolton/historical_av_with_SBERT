May 5th 1867

Monterey, Va.

Friend Jackson

I inquired of Mr. Slaven in regard to the trial of freedmen, for horse thievery &c during the war, and find that [unclear: men] were tried. There was a man by the name of (a U.S. scout) tried on some such charge and he has appealed to a "Military Tribunal"

Remember me to Mrs. Jackson and F.S. Tukey and family.

Yours truly

C. B. Fessenden

There is not a horse to be hired in this and I am

afraid, not to be A to die in this. C.B.F.
