Col. John W. Jordan
A.S.A.Comr
Staunton, Va

April 21, 1868

Winchester,

Colonel

Your communication of 18th inst. reporting your arrival at Staunton and asking instructions has been received.

No instructions beyond those contained in the files of Circulars and orders in your office are deemed necessary.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt Servant

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr

Any blanks, stationery &c required, can be obtained on application to this office
