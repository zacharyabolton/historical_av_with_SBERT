Mr. Thos P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Va

Nov. 19t 1867

Winchester, Va.

Sir

Please inform me whether David Collins, referred to in your communications of Aug 28th & Sept 9t last, has obtained a divorce from his wife, or whether he has taken any steps to procure one.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt St

John A. McDonnell
Capt. & S.A.C.
