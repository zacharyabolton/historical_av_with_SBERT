October 2nd 1870

John D. Imboden Esq

Take notice that we will move the Judge of the Circuit Court of Augusta County, on The 10th Day of the next November term of said court to dissolve the injunction granted upon your motion on the 5th day of April 1870 by the Judge of said Court, against the Central Bank of Virginia & ourselves.

Wm. H. Shins Trustee
B.B. Dounghe
H.M. Bell Trustee &-
