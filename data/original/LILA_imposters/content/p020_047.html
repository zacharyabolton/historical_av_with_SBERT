May 2nd, 1865

Sir:

You are hereby detached from the Naval-School, and leave is granted you to visit your home.

You will report by letter to the Hon ble Secretary of the Navy as soon as practicable.

Paymaster Wheless will issue you ten days rations, and all Quarter-Masters are requested to furnish you with transportation.

Respectfully,
Yr. obt. servant,

Wm. H. Parker
Commandant
By order of the
Secretary of the Navy
Mid n R. H. Fleming
C.S.N.
