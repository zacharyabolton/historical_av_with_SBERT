Capt J A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
Nov 23/68

Staunton

Capt: -

Mary Peters (c) of Staunton complains that her husband Wm Peters deserted her about one year ago & left their child now about 3 years old for her to support & she finds herself unequal to the task.

Cannot this man be made to support his child? He is now living in service at Hill's Hotel in Harrisonburg Va

Very respectfully

Roswell Waldo
A.S.A. Comr
