Dec 17. 68

Winchester,

Sir

In reply to your letter of 14 inst. this day received, you are respectfully informed that the [unclear: Gt] & : Coats (15 each) Invoiced to you by Maj White are the same as these sent by Maj. Brown & that your receipt has been cancelled and the Invoices returned to Maj. White, they having already been receipted for by me, tho' [unclear: incorrectly] described.

All the clothing you are expected to acct for is that enumerated in my letters of 14t & 15t inst.

As certificates that this has been properly acct of & an examination of accts has to be made before final payment, it is important that all [unclear: asss ] should be sent in as early as 25t as mentioned in my previous letter.

Very Respectfully

John A. McDonnell
