Mr Th. P. Jackson

Feby 20th 1868

Waynesboro

Dear Sir

You did not come on tuesday as you promised me. Those negroes are still in their and want possession of the house. As I told I gave them 3 weeks notice one I hired only by the week and settle up with her the other one has been staying their she has good opportunities to hire for more than I gave her but she thinks I think that the will draw a support from the government but she is able support herself you will accomdate me by removing them immediately and you oblige

yours &c

John K. Keiser
