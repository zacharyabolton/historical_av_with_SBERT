H. S. Evans, A.A.G.

Aug 27 1865

Staunton, Va

Dear Sir

Will you have the kindness to inform me where I shall direct a Letter so it will reach Prof. Woodbury, also to inform me what the arrangements are for the transportation of Teachers from their homes to their respective places of duty, that is whether they travel on all [unclear: Rounds] not Govt at half fare.

If you will do this you will greatly oblige your

Humble Servant

F.S. Tukey
Asst Supt Freedmen &c
