Charles S. Arnold
Agent Va. C. R. R. Staunton

Ap 20/67

Staunton Va.

Sir

A colored man named ____ Whiting is reported to me as "employed as foreman upon a Locomotive on the Virginia Central R.R." and living at Staunton. Does such a man appear on your rolls, and if so when can I seem him?

Yr obt servt
Thos P. Jackson
Agent
