Col. O. Brown, Asst. Comr.
Richmond, Va
Sept 1st 1865

Staunton, Va.

Colonel

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your copies each of the blank reports of Refugees &c & of Lands, and in answer to the request to forward the Land Rept by the 5th inst have respectfully to inform you that I have no Lands or Houses to report.

I am, Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obedt Servt

W. Storer How, Capt & AQM
Supt 6th Dist Va
