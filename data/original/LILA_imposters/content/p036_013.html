Bvt Maj. F. N. Clark
Washington, D.C.

February 29th 1868

Parkersburg, W. Va.

Dear Sir

In reference to your communication dated 20th ult. concerning Cynis Bannister, I will state that I visited him this morning, read him your letter. He stated that there had been some difficulty between them which induced him to leave, has been absent about three years.

I endeavored to impress upon him the importance of caring for his children. He directed me to say that he intended to return this spring, perhaps about a month hence.

Very Respectfully

(Sgd) S. E. Colburn

Official
F.N. Clark, A.A.A.G.
