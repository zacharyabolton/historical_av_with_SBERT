Nov. 3rd 1859

Arbor Hill

To: Jno. H. McCue
Dear Sir

I wish you would send me ten Dollars. It being the remainder of what you owe me. i am very much in need of money now. I am informed you are doing very well in Nelson Co. which i am glad to hear. wright me on recipt of this. let me know if you are flush. Send the ten along

yours. Respectfully

David Speck

I will Recpt. D. S.
