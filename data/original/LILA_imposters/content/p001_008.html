Sara A. Hotchkiss

My Dear --

Let Nelson take the hinges and lock off of my map box, up stairs, to put on my box, and you can send me the key in a letter. Nelson will find some way to get it to me -- Paste on it the following direction -- Jed. Hotchkiss Top. Eng. Lt. Gen. Jackson's Staff 2nd Corps, Army of N. Va.Care of Maj. W. J. Hawks Guinea Station R.F. & P.R.R.
