Mr. Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton Va.

Augt. 19th 1867

Winchester, Va.

Sir

In reply to your inquiry concerning estimates of rations you are informed that no more estimates need be sent, unless you find it necessary to issue rations to prevent actual suffering among the freedmen.

It will be understood that no rations are required if no estimate is received here by the 20th of the previous month.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Svt.

John A. McDonnell
Capt & S.A.C.
