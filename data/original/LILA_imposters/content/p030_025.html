July 23rd, 1867

New Berne N.C.

Respectfully Returned: Although diligent Search and inquiry have been made for the within named persons No trace of them has been found in New Berne N.C. (In explanation of the delay in returning this paper I beg and leave to state that I have been on sick leave for the three weeks past)

A. Coats
1st Lieut V.R.C. in charge
