April 25 1868

Richmond,

Sir

Mr. John H. Richardson will pay you fourteen 08/100 dollars amt. due by the Va Central RailRd Co. to H. Holland for work in the month of September 1867, as appears by the payroll.

I find that the amt which was due to H. Holland for work done in August 1867 = five 36/100 dollars was paid to H [unclear: Giles] to the payroll for that month & the [unclear: work] of H Holland to Giles filed therewith.

I will thank you to receipt the indorsed order for the sum herewith sent & return it to me by Mr. Richardson

Respectfully &c

J. Garnett, Jr.

[on seperate sheet]VIRGINIA CENTRAL RAILROAD.
Capt. J. H. Richardson will please pay to J Jordan Agt Bureau at Staunton the $14 08 due H Holland & take his receipt therefor in the enclosed order & bring it to me on his return. I enclose the money to pay him.
[signed]JG
