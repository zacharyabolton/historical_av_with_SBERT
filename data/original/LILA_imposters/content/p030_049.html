Mr. Nathan Greaver

Aug 5 1867

Staunton, Va.

Sir

Paul Madison has made complaint you refuse to pay him $8 20/100 balance of account for coal.

You may save trouble by calling at this office and making arrangements for speedy payment.

Thos P. Jackson
Agent

[note by Jackson] Paul Madison called & states that N. Greaver has a to settle for him back [unclear: home] &c
