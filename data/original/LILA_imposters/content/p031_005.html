Mr. O'Brien
McGaheysville
Rockingham Co

Aug 13 1867

Staunton, Va.,

Sir

Please inform me if you know or can learn anything of Joseph and Caroline Tell or Terrel (cold) who live at McGaheysville and if they can receive their daughter Rosetta Tell or Terrel, who is here out of health & doing poorly. An early reply will oblige.

Yr obt servt
Thos P. Jackson
