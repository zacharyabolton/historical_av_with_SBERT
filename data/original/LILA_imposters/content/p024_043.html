Mr W. E. Whiting

May 11 1866

Dear Sir

Please say to Mr Montross that instead of nine, there are twelve Lamps with an extra set of Chimneys they are not Brackets but a glass wall lamp with reflectors. Please inform me what to do with them.

I am with much respect Your Abt. Servant

F. S. Tukey Asst. Supt. B. R. F. + A. Lands
