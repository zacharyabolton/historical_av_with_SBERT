Nov. 18/57

Staunton

Dear Sir,

Enclosed you have statement of your act showing amot. due me this date of $68 54/100.

I never was harder run for money than I am at this time.-- A note of mine from Philda. will fall due in bank in a few days & I lack considerably of the amot. to pay it with.

Now if you can let me have the above amot., you will confer a great favor on

Yours Respectfully

Robt. Cowan
