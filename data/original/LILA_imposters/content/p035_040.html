Feb the 4th 1868

Waynesboro

Sir Mr Jackson

I [unclear: mean] to inform that i have 4 small children My husband is Dead 18 months i am in a very Destitute Condition I am laid Up with rheumatism and Unable to work for them you wold extend a great charity to assist me.

Respectfully

Mary Fay

Please sir direct to the care of Mr. Benjamin Lyons, Waynesboro Augusta Co. Va
Cannot Assist TPJ
