Surgeon J. J. DeLamater, U.S.S.
Surgeon in Chief B.R.F. & A.L.

April 5th, 1867

Richmond, Va.

Sir:

I have the honor to request transportation to Staunton Va. for Ben Beal, freedman a patient in this Hospital successfully operated on for Cataract.

Staunton, Va. is his home and if he is transported there, the Government will be relieved of his support.

Very respectfully
Your Obed't Serv't

D.R. Brower
A.A. Surgeon in Charge
Howard's Grove Hospital
