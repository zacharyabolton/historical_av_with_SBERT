Capt Jno A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
Winchester Va
Nov. 23/68

Staunton

Capt: -

I have the honor to request to be informed if the school report to be submitted by Circular 26. from Richmond Oct 21/68 - is to include those schools taught under the auspices of local church societies as well as by A Miss Assn. Also if the form on back of said Circular is to be considered merely as a guide by which a form of proper dimensions is to be made up - or is said report to be submitted on back of the Circular?

Very respectfully

Roswell Waldo
A.S.A. Comr
