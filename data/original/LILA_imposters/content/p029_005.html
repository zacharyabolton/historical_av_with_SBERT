Mr. Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Va

June 3d 1867

Winchester, Va.,

Sir

No reports from your Division have yet been received. There ought to be no reason why your reports cannot be sent in promptly at the close of the month. The receipts for supplies furnished destitute whites are particularly needed by the commissary to complete his Returns.

You will please forward your reports forthwith, and hereafter send them in with promptness.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Sevt

John A. McDonnell
Capt & S.A.C.
