April 21. 1861

Head Quarters 13th Brigade VM
Harpers Ferry

Sir

I am satisfied the report of my [unclear: aid] in reference to the three deserters from the command of Lt Jones U. S. A. is true.

I enclose questions propounded to Davis who deserted from Lt Jones Command & is now a [unclear: Sr ] in the Camps of Capt. Geo. W. Chambers, said to be in your command but not attached to my command.

I have the honor to be with
the highest respect
yr obt servt

M H. Harison
Brg Genl
Comdg Genl
