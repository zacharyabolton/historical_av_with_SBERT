July 10, 1867

Staunton, Va

States that Marian Hall (Fr) claims possession of his daughter Meary Elizabeth, bound by County Court 9 yrs ago. to S. C. Slaven of Bath Co -- Hall being a ______ _______ keep and provide for his daughter by a free wife. Asks that the records of Bath Co. be examined to see if the indenture is recorded and if it will be regarded as binding by the Bureau.

Jackson Tho. P., Agent
