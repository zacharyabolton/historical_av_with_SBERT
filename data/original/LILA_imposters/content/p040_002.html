Nov 19th 1868

Staunton

My Dear Mollie

I looked for a letter from you this Morning my love but was sadly disapointed I wrote you by Hamie on last Monday but he did not leave untill Tuesday and I felt Confident that I would get a letter this Morning from you My love I will not blame you for I Know it was not your fault for if they had of bin a reglar mail I Know I would gotten a letter from you. I do hope that your Pa will have a Post office Stablished when he returns home Ada Recd a letter from him on Yesterday he Stated in the letter that he would be home the latter part of this week provided he left New York on Monday.

My love I will be up Saturday if I live.

Magnus is talking of Coming up with me I hope he will I Cant say for Sertian that he will come for you Know Magnus. Ada Sad last night that she was going to write to her Ma that I was a frade to Come up Saturday you tell your Ma that I am coming if I live & that I will take all the blame on my Self. My love I love you with true devotion of my heart & Soul, I love you with unchanging devotion heart soul mind & Strenth and Ever shall as long as I live you are the only one in this world I love I Can not Express my love for you My only love of this Earth. All are well all send love I Send a loveing Kiss you. My love to your Ma Hamie to all. I love you with my whole heart

Your Loveing

D H Evans
