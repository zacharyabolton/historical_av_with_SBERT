Mrs Virginia L Miller.
Little york
Penn

august 23 1864

Camp Chase ohio

Mrs Virginia Miller

having rote to you some time ago in regard as to some clothing and as yet Have not received them and have come to the conclusion you did not get it the letter I also have learned that another man had rote to you also I Thought in all probability he had interfered when I rote to you they wer no prisoners in this camp knew your address But myself and the Verry Evening I rote To you I understand That They was annothers letters wrote I want you to write to me and let me know if you got my letters or not and if that man who you receved a letters from interfered or not you will please answer This letter soon and let me know what you can do in regard as to my wants with these few lines I close Hoping to hear from you soon if you send me these clothing Send them in care of Col Richardson commanding & By Express

please answer this soon

Yours Truly

Franklin Carroll Prison B camp chase ohio

P S pleas send me some stamp
