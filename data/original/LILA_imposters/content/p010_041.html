June the 12 1862

Camp near [unclear: Wirs Cove]

Dear Sister

It has been so long since I have written to you I dont know when was the last time. I wrote to [unclear: Est] yesterday eavining and I believe I gave her all the news I knowed only I want you to send me a shirt and a pair of cotton pants in the same size off my Sunday pants. I would like to have them as soon as you can send them. I [unclear: ort ] to have a Calico shirt at home I think. I would like to have something good to Eat if you could send something down. I have goten tired of bread and meat. I want to get home about time if I can.

I have nothing more to write this time.

George W. Baylor

to Mary C. Baylor
