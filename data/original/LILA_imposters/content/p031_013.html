Aug 24th 1867

Woodstock, Va

Friend Jackson

May I again trouble you? Please send the enclosed letter to Co. Clk requesting an immediate reply. Also request Mr. Isaac Crebs of Augusta Co to furnish such statement of his claim against the Pitman estate, and such facts in the case as he may be disposed.

Respectfully,
Your Friend

J.T.H. Hall
Lt. VRC & Mil Cmr

Please answer as soon as convenient JH
