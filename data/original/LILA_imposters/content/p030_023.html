July 22nd /67

Waynesboro

Mr. Jackson

I talked to Mr. Morrison and wife this morning he said that if they had an doubt he could show anyone that he could give a good right to the lot, and if they are not certefied they can take the building off when they get ready.

Yours &c

John L. A. Crawford
Trustee

P.S. Baldwin is not to blame, he was overpowered by the balance of the Committee. It is out of his power at this time for him to come to see you but as soon as he can he will come.
