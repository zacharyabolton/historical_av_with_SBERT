Col. O. Brown Assist Commissioner
Richmond Va
Oct 5th 1865

Staunton Va

Colonel

In obedience to orders from your office, which I presume are awaiting me at Winchester, Va, I have respectfully to inform you that I shall at once remove the Head Quarters of this District to Winchester where I will [unclear: probably] arrive on the evening of Saturday October 7th 1865

I am Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How, Capt & AQM
Supt Sixth Dist Va
