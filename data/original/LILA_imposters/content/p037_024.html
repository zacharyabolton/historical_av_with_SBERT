Messrs Ross C. Eakle
And all whom it may concern
Mt. Sidney,
Augusta Co Va

May 16, 1868

Staunton, Va.,

Gent

You are respectfully requested to give to the Rev. Mr. King the use of the M. E. Church at the above named point for religious services on Sunday May 17, 1868 at 3:30 P.M.

It is earnestly hoped that no demonstrations will be made interfering with the peace and good order of the neighborhood tomorrow.

Respectfully
Your obd. svt

Jno W. Jordan
Asa Coms &c
