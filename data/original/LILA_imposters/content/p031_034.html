Mr. Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Va.

Sept 5th 1867

Winchester, Va.,

Sir

The following information is desired immediately, at this office, viz: the State in which the following parties were born, Thos P. Jackson, Agent, and Charles W. Teagle, Messenger.

Also the name of the State in which each of them resided at the time of appointment in this Bureau.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt St

John A. McDonnell
Capt & S.A.C.
