Dec 15t 1868

Winchester

Sir

In addition to the clothing enumerated in my communication of yesterday, I have just received Invoices for 40 Petticoats, shipped you from Richmond. You will please include them in your issues.

Very Respectfully
Your Obt Svt

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
