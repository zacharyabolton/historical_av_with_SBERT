Officer of the Bureau Department

March 21st/68

Near Staunton Va.

Kind Sir

This woman came to you in trouble. Please hear her statement and give her the advise and information she asks. You can rely on what she tells you. I have had her employed for the last fifteen months and know her to be an honest, truthful & virtuous woman.

Very Respectfully,

Sallie Myers

[Written in Jackson's hand] "Esther Smith (c) has 4 children by Albert Smith (c) who now lives at [unclear: Chilberth] Ohio with a wife. Deserted Esther in 1863 & has done nothing for her since. Now wants to claim two oldest children. TPJ"
