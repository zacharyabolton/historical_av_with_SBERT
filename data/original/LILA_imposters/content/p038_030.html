Col. J. W. Jordan
A.S.A.Comr
Staunton, Va.
Augt. 27 1868

Winchester

Colonel

Inclosed please find Vouchers for Rents & Service in your Division for present month, and for purchases for September.

Please return the same receipted. No blanks have yet been received from Capt. White, nor any instructions on the subject of officers pay, and the [unclear: a--s] are therefore made in the old way for this month.

Very Respectfully
Your Obedt Servant

John A McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
