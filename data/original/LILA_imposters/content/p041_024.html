Febry 9th 1869

Staunton

My Dear Mollie

Home Safely a gain. My love I will look for you down on Thursday for Sertian with your Pa & Di dont disappoint us for we will look for you. Ella was so much disappointed this morning that you & Di did not come I will be at the train Thursday morning My love if I live. I would liked of Stayed longer with you if I could have done so for when I go to see you I all wayes dislike to leave you for I would rather be with you my love at your home then any other place in the world. I thought this morning when I Started I would be left. I ran nerly all the way to the Gap I had plenty time.

I will look for you down Thursday my love for Sertian. I love you with the same unchanging devotion of my heart & Soul & ever Shall while I live. I love you my love & no other in this would you were my first & only love of my heart & Soul

I cant write you but a Short note this time my love after I got home I had some Business to attend to that Keep me Bussy till nerly car time. give my love to your Ma & Pa Di Lolo & Ham tell Lolo I will answer her question & then I have one to ask her My love come Thursday for Sertian. I will look for you I love you with my whole heart & Soul mind & Strenth.

Ella & family are all well Send love to you all & will look for you all Down Thursday. I Send a loveing Kiss to you my love & love you with my whole heart.

Yours Loving

D H Evans
