Rev. E. P. Smith

May 20th 1867

Dear Sir

Enclosed herewith please find my transportation papers returned for correction. Instead of reading enroute from Staunton Va. To Greenwich [unclear: Mass.] it should be from Staunton Va. To Calais Maine.

When arriving at N. Y, it should be from N. Y. to New London thence from New London to Boston. thence from Boston to Portland Me. Thence from Portland to Eastport. thence from Eastport to Calais.

Very Respectfully

Mrs Annie A. Dunn
