To Thomas P. Jackson Agt
Bu R F and AL
Staunton Va.

May 29th 1867

Richmond Va

Sir

I shipped you the following Articles of Sub Stores or rations for Destitute Freedmen in the month of June

(200) Two Hundred pounds of Pork
(400) Four Hundred pounds of Herrings
(525) Five Hundred and Twenty five pounds C. Meal
(196) One Hundred and Ninety six pounds flour

I am Sir
Respectfully your
Obt Servt

Herman Schreiner
1st Lt 20th Inf
per [unclear: Joseph] Clerk
