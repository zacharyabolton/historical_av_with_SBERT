R. M. Manly

Dec 19, 1866

Staunton Va

Dear Sir

Your [unclear: Mission] was received the 17th and in reply will beg that there must be some mistake somewhere, but where I am unable to say -- the Whole number enrolled and the average no in attendance are the most important questions. will [unclear: ans]. The whole number of Colored pupils enrolled is 360, average attendence last month 230. If I was able to write more I would give you full particulars concerning our school. I am most sick [unclear: a bit]. Will write you again soon.

With much respect
I am sir your obt serv

J. H. Piper
