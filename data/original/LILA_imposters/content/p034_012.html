Tho P. Jackson
Dec. 16 1867

Harpers Ferry W. Va.

Dear Friend

I am sorry to trouble you with so many letters for no purpose. I was detained so long in Washington, that I could not get to Staunton, so I have come back to take a fresh start.

If there are letters or dispatches in your care for me, Please forward them inst. greatly oblige.

Yours Truly

N. C. Brackett
