Mr. Jackson

June the 15th

Waynesboro

Sir

I rite to inform you that I am suffering for evrything that support life. I have neather food nor raiment. I have bin beging for too years and the citizens are getting tyard of my beging and I am verry old and infirm and unless I receive help from you or some other souce I can not possible get along.

yours respectfully

Feby Leach
