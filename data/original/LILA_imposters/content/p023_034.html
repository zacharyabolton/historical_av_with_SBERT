Clerk of the Court of Claims
Washington, D.C.

Feby 12th 1866

Sir

The accompanying claim has been handed me by Robert Brick a Freedman, to be forwarded as it now is, but I have as yet been unable to learn anything concerning claims of Freedpeople previously forwarded.

I should be glad to learn receive any information that would tend to assist the Freedmen in obtaining compensation for losses sustained by them.

I am, Sir &c

W. Storer How
Capt &c
