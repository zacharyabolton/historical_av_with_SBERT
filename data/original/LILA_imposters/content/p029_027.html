Capt. John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
Winchester

June 23 1867

Staunton, Va.,

Captain

I respectfully ask permission to engage a clerk at this office until completion of Registration and Elections in Augusta County. The business of 4th Div. is very large and the Freedmen widely scattered and together with the duties of Registering officers I find I am not able single handed to keep up effectively with [unclear: outdoor] and office work

Yr. obt. servt
Thos P. Jackson
Agent
