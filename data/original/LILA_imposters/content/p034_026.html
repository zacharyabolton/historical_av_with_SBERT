Mr. Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
Staunton, Va

Dec. 30th 1867

Winchester, Va

Sir

I have the honor to transmit herewith one 70/100 dollars in postage stamps in payment of your account for postage for the present month.

Hereafter you will make out your postage account on form 20. (Q.M.D.) and forward the same at the end of each quarter, to Capt. Geo. Q. White, C.Q.M. & D.O. Bureau R.F. & A.L., Richmond, Va.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Servant

John A. McDonnell
Capt. & S.A.C.
