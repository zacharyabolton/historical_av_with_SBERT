Mr. Tho. P. Jackson
A.S.A.C.
Staunton, Va.

March 19, 1868

Winchester

Sir,

I have the honor to inform you that the Assistant Commissioner has authorized the expenditure of $40 in repair of schoolhouse at Laurel Hill, upon the trustees -- the security referred to in your application. Blank for purchases of material & services are enclosed. Please have them receipted in blank, and return them with a memorandum on a separate sheet, of the labor performed and materials purchased; they should be returned in time to reach this office three or four days before the expiration of the month in which the purchases are made or services rendered.

Very Respectfully

John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst. Commr
