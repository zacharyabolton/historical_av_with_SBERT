Col. O. Brown, Assist Comr
Richmond Va.

March 22 1866

Winchester, Va.

Colonel

I have the to forward herewith a copy of the "Valley Virginian" newspaper published at Staunton Va inviting attention to the article headed "A boy fined twenty dollars for whipping a man" and respectfully requesting that it may be submitted as corroboration of my [unclear: inducement] on the papers relating to the case alluded which were forwarded through your office to Department Head Quarters.

I am Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How
Bt Maj AQM & Supt
