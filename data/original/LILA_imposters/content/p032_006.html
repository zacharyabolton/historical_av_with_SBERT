Mrs Martha Ryan
Parkersburg W.Va

Sep 23 1867

Staunton, Va.,

Madame

Can you give me any information as to the whereabouts of William Brown (c) son of Grayson Brown of Staunton. William left Staunton in 1865. If you cannot give the information, please state if any other person at Parkersburg can do so.

Yr. obt. servt
Tho P Jackson
Agent
