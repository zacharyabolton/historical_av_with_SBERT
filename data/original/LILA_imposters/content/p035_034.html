Thos P. Jackson [unclear: Esq]
Staunton

Feb 2d 1868

Augusta Alum

Dr Sir

Your favor of Jan 29th is recd. The boy Jesse commenced work for me on the 8th of Oct last, having agreed to work by the month from that date @ $8.00 per mon; Sometime before his month was out he ran away without giving me any notice or leaving me with anyone to supply his place thus breaking his contract & depriving himself of all claim on me. I was away during the most of his stay here & my clerk informs me that he was utterly worthless & did not earn his board

Very Respectfully

J.A. Hefelfinger
