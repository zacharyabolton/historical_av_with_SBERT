Thomas P. Jackson

September 11, 1867

Respectfully returned to Mr. Thos. P. Jackson, agent.

The only way in which Collins can obtain a legal separation from his wife is to sue for a divorce. To erase the name from the Register, while, it could not be authorized, would not alter the fact of his being legally married to her. The registrat ion is only an evidence of marriage. He would be legally married, whether registered or not.

John A McDonnell, Capt A. S. A. C.
