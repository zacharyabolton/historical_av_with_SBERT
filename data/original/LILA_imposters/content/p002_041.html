Thomas P. Jackson
Staunton, Va

Winchester Va

Dear Sir

I have learned from Capt. McDonal that the Registers ware not appointed in Staunton Mr. Stitt & I are from the State of Pensyvania we are living in Winchester at present we have boath been in Pa. Recty & if they are not appointed please let us know & by you doing so you will oblidge us by doing so.

Adres with kindest wished

William Stitt
