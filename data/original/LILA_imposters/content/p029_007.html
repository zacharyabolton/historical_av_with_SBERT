Capt John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr

June 3rd 1867

Winchester

Captain

I have the honor to transmit Report to Bureau Affairs Criminal Affairs (Freedmen) and School affairs for this Division of Your District In relation to the case of Common v. Hill I respectfully remark that Defdts friends are not satisfied with the conviction on the evidence addressed but his counsel declines taking any steps to set aside the judgement and await action from Hill through his personal friend before making any detailed or protest to you

Your obt Servt

Thos P Jackson
Agent
