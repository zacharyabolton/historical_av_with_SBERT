Richmond
Feb 1st 1866

Staunton, Va

Capt.

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of order no. 238 for destitute freedpeople, from Staunton to Richmond. I should have done so ere this, but that the matter escaped my attention.

I am Captain,
Your obt. srvt.
