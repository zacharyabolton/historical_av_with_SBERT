Mr D. Evans

August 22nd 1868

Bridgewater Va

Dear Sir!

We have three of your Tobacco Boxes--Empty--perfectly Sound-- what will you Ask in cash per lb to fill the three boxes-- with the Same quality of Tobacco we have been purchasing of you if you will fill them at the proper figures--we will put them in a case and send them to your Store by a wagon from this place--let us hear--

Yours at

Byrd & Sibert
R Sibert
