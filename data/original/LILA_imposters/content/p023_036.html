Col. O. Brown, Assist Comr
Richmond, Va.
Feby 14th 1866

Winchester, Va.

Colonel

I have the honor to inform you that I shall start today on a tour through the upper portion of my District, reaching Staunton, perhaps, on the 17th of the month, and respectfully request that any business requiring immediate attention may be directed to me at that place until about the 24th of this month.

Capt. G. W. Wells, agent and Asst Supt will [unclear: transact] the remaining office business here until my return.

I am Colonel
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How, Capt & AQM
Supt Sixth Dist, Va
