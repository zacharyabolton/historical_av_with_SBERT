Dated Staunton Oct. 9. 1866.

Received at Buhd " 9

To Brig. Genl Horace Neide
Hd Qrs Dept Potomac

A law student named J C Johnson shot a freedman named Patrick Thompson on Sunday P.M. - freedman died the day following. Johnson has made his escape six (6) feet high slender fair skin no beard black hair age about twenty (20) years Resides Greedwood Mississippi was here this A.M. will be apt to take Tenn RR at Lynchburg.

G.B. Carse
Bt Col.
