Brevt Maj W. S. How, AQM
Supt Sixth Dist Va

March 22/66

Winchester, Va

Maj

I have the honor to request that transportation be furnished the Freedwoman Martha Coleman from Staunton to Richmond she being now a dependant upon the Bureau and having friends in Richmond that will provide for her.

I am Major
Very Respectfully
Your obt Servt

John P. How
Asst Supt
Sub Dist 'D'

[in different hand] Transportation order No 453 issued dated 28 March 1866
