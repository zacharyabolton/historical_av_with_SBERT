Col. O. Brown
Asst. Comr.
Richmond, Va
Dec. 16th 1865

Winchester

Colonel

I have the honor to transmit herewith my Report of Rations Issued in the month of November 1865.

This report has been unavoidably retained for correction and is now incomplete in not giving the values of the ration, because it could not be obtained from the commissaries at Winchester and Staunton.

I am &c

W.S.H.
Capt &c
