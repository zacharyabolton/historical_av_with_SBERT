Capt. John A. McDonnell
Sub Asst Comr
Winchester
Ap 24/1867

Staunton Va

Captain

I respectfully forward claim of Reily McDonald, Staunton against C.W. Oltmann, late of Staunton but now said to be at Lexington (at Washington College in employ of one Col. Allen. Oltmann gave McDonald an order on Mr. Jed. Hotchkiss of Staunton but he refuses to pay for the reason he has no funds of Oltmann in hand and so states in writing.

Yr obt servt
Thos P. Jackson
Agent
