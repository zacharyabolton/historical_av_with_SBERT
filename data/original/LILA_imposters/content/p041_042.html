March 30 1869

Staunton

My dear friend

I have your letter. I regret I did not answer you as freely on the subject of that suit as you desired. I now send you the statement_ I cannot think of the proceeding with its surroundings without getting excited, but it is over now. the deed is done. If 15 is a sufficient reward for such unkindness-almost [unclear: undeserved]-let it be

We have nothing astir here. I handed your letter to Judge Sheffey Judge Fultz strides as stately as a Prince since the judicial robes have fallen on him-- [unclear: Bennette] + Ryan are inoffice under the style + name of Cline + Sons. no change is observed except in the to

I am as ever
Yours Truly

Have you done anything for our widow's soldiers claim? How is an old soldier is dead intestate ?
