Lieut. J.T.H. Hall

September 2nd, 1867

Staunton, Va.

Lieutenant

I have forwarded by this day's mail a request (with accompanying papers) on behalf of John Gum praying intervention of Military authorities to prevent sale of his property under execution in hands of William Somers, sheriff and a stay of proceedingsin thre other suits againsts him of a similar character which come up at next term (Sept 12/67-- On the 17th day of July (L13 49 v2/67) I addressed Lt. Col. Franklin on behalf of James D. Hick, also of Highland Co for whose arrest on a similar charge the Sheriff (Somers) hold a warrant but which he has not executed and notified him Somers (July 17/67) Hick had appealed to the Military authorities & his arrest would be at Somers' peril. Please let me have some information of probable action in both cases before 10th [?] as I propose to attend Court at [?] on the 12th September.

Yr. Obt. Servt.

Thos. P. Jackson
Agent
