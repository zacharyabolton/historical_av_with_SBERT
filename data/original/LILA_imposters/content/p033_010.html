Capt Jackson
Nov. 8th 1867

Micham's River

Dear Sir

Should you receive a letter from Mr Elmsly or should one come to Staunton for me, to your care or to the care of Mrs Branham, please forward to me at Michum's River, Albemarle Va care of M.W. Wallace Esqr. I am waiting here to hear from him before going to Baltimore. Please hand the enclosed note to [unclear: Cush--] & Peck & oblige.

Yours Respectfully

Elizabeth Elmsly
