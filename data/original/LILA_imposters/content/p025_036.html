Mr Henry Ott

Sept 18th 1866

Midway

Dear Sir

Yours of 15th Just at hand Regarding nails we have none on hands I except some 7. P Nelson Bot no nails, said he wuld not buy them to sell here at the price use your own descresion about the nails. there is no prooffit in them. You will find some goods at Millers wool dark grey for Ladies Sacks or drepes Nelson Bot at 30 or 35 cents they had some at 25 cents. he thinks Miller's is the place. Get several pieces of goods for mens wear low price & dark coulor

yours H

O A Spencer & Co

Buy for Nelson 10 or 16 pounds A Allspice & pack with ours
