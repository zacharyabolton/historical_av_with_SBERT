William B. Gallaher

Jan 20 1865

Richmond Arsenal

Sir,

Your letter of the 19th was delivered to me this morning by your brother.

I enclose a new contract for leather/4 copies/which please sign in the presence of a witness and return to me.

I forwarded to your adress, several days ago the contract for the additional number of Hides, with a proviso with regard to loss by the public enemy. I am at a loss to account for it's not having reached you.

Very respectful

Jas. Dinwiddie
Jas. Dinwiddie
Capt Ch Conts
W. Macdormish
