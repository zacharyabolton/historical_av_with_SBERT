Bt. Brig. Gen. O. Brown
Asst Comr, Va
Richmond, Va
Dec. 31s 1867

Winchester, Va.

General

I have the honor to report that in the 1st Division of this Sub District about fifty able bodied freedmen, for whom employment cannot be found. In the other Divisions the demand for labor is still equal to the supply.

Very Respectfully
Your Obdt Servant

John A. McDonnell
Capt & S.A.C.
