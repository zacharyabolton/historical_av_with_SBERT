Bt Brig Genl O. Brown
Assist Commissioner Richmond Va

April 30th 1866

Winchester Va

General

In accordance with [unclear: P] VI Circular No 10 dated Richmond Va March 12th 1866 I have the honor to report that no information has reached me [unclear: tending] to show that by restoring to the State authorities jurisdiction in Criminal Cases on colored persons.

They have been treated otherwise than with impartiality and fairness the law respecting their testimony carried out in good faith with the exception of the failure to arrest the murderer of a freedman at Berryville and the gross injustice to a freedman by a Magistrate at Staunton previously reported.

I am General
Very Respectfully
Your Obedient Servant

W. Storer How
Bt Major agent & Supt
