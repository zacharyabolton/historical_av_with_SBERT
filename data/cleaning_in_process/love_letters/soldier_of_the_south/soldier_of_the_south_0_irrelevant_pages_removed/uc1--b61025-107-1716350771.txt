In which General Pickett Pleads an Order
OBRA

LD Peter is to go to Tennessee to reënforce
Bragg. He has placed his plans before the Sec
retary of War.
.
Now, my darling, I have just had a long pow.
wow with him (Old Peter) who, 'old war horse'
as he is, has been in love himself, is still in love,
will always be in love, and knows of our love — of
our plighted troth — and knowing it, tells me it is
his purpose to take me with him on this proposed
expedition.
Now, my Sallie, your soldier is a soldier, and never,
even to himself, questions an order. "His not to reason
why.' Darling, do you know what this means? Why,
my little one, it means that you haven't one moment's
respite. It means that you are to be Mrs. General
George E. Pickett, my precious wife, right away. It
means that you are to fufill your promise to come to
me at a moment's notice'. Yours, too, now, not to
