YULE LOG
all de flames er de bark, en de splinters
en all de li'l' logs put tergedder ter
mek hit bu'n out, ef dey could,
endurin' de time dat Chris' mus las's.
"Ole Missis (Marse Gin'ul Pickett's
ma) use' ter say dat dem sparks er de
Yule log wuz de li'l' splinters dat
splunt off de Star in de East dat time
at Beflehem w'en all ter a sudden de
Star come ter a stop-still ter show de
wise mens Him whar come ter sabe 'em.
En de ser'us ole Yule log lays dar, a
smoulderin' en a smoulderin' all thoo
de whole er de Chris'mus times, en a
shroudin' wid hit's w'ite ashes hit's
own gre't, 'roun', red, glowin' heart-
dat heart er fiah dat's allers true ter
Chris' mus.
