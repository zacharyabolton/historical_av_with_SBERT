'pon de groun' mos' all de yeah 'roun'

'sepn' de ber'es dat growed 'pon de li'l'
bushes en de roots dat growed in de
groun' en de nuts dat drapped offn de
trees.

he'd been a libin' fer mo' dana hundud
er a t'ousand yeahs. He wuz so ole
dat he wuz all scrooched up wid rheu-

en his back wuz drawed double. He
wuz so blin' he couldn' skasely see

ruckled en doubled up wid de miz'ry
