WUZ SANTA CLAUS
"She is your mother," he said.
"Go with her, but whenever you want
to come back to us you shall come."
"Nuttin' else but you 'cidin' lak
dat is onsettled my 'terminashun,
Marse Da'. 'Member, now, don' no-
body but you sont me away, Marse Da',
en me dat you allers called yo' 'yeah-
corn,' 'count er yo' fin'in' me in de
husks, en me jes' lak yo' own chile
dat you-all done raise fum a baby. En
Miss Lizzy stan'in' dar en ain't sayin'
nuttin' 'tall fer ter keep me fum gwine
needer, en Marse Eddie en de chillun
I wuz brung up wid, en Miss Sallie's
chillun dat I he'p ter bring up myse'f,
en eben down ter de dorgs en de yudder
t'ings on de plantation you's mekin'
me leabe en g'long whar I don' know
nuttin' ner nobody ner nuttin' ner no-
