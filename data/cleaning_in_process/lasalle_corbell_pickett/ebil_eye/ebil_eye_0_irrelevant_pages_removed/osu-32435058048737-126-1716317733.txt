EBIL EYE
You
whar would you be ef hit 'twan't fer
de Ole Man. I'll tell you whar you'd
be, fer I's a closet man myse'f, en I'll
gib you de stret answer dat is writ down
in de Scripshun 'boutn yo' whar'bouts.
You wouldn' hab no whar'bouts.
wouldn' be yer, at ali, en ef you wuz yer
you wouldn' be wuff a pinch er snuff
wid all de sneeze part er de snuff dried
outn hit. W'y, you ax-w'y wouldn'
you be yer? Kaze you'd be bruck all
ter flinders en be flung 'pon de fertile
heap 'In de Miz.' Dat's w'y.
"My belubbed brederin, de Scripshun
mobeth us in sundry places, en says de
Lawd meked de ye'th en de sea en all
dat 'In de Miz,' en dat den He res'
Hisse'f de Sebenf day en hollowed hit.
