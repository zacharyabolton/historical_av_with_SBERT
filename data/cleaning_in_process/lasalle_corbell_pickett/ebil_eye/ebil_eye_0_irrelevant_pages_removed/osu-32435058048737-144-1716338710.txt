EBIL EYE
haint nobody else ebber saw hit. De
Lawd, my brederin, is de onlyes' one
dat ebber seed de light dat He meked
on de firs' day.
En
"Ona Chuzed'y, my belubbed brederin,
He meked de fundament, en diwided
all de t'ings up inter diff'unt parts, en
He saw ag'in, en ag'in He say 'hit wuz
good.' En on Wenzd'y, w'en He
sow'd de seeds, en planted de trees en all
de inguns, en cabbages, en brack-eye
peas en punkins en sweet taters en all
de yudder kin's er craps, en dey all tuck
root en come up, en had a good stand
en growed, en He saw ag'in, en ag'in
He say 'hit wuz good.' En on a Thuzd'y,
w'en Hemeked de Two Lights en sot em
bofe up in de Fundament,-one gre't big
blazin' Light ter shine by de daytime,
en a whole passel er li'l' lights ter shine
