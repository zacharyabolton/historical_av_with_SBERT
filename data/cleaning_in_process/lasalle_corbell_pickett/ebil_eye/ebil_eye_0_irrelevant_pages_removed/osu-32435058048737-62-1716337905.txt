EBIL EYE
wuz gwine ter disgrace en ruint myse'f.
De Senator's wife she griebed lak I wuz
her own sister. She say dat she'd
druther see me daid en in my grabe dan
ter th'o' myse'f 'way on dat common,
low-life nigger, en she en all her frien's,
en all de Senator's frien's, too, tried dar
ve'y lebel bes' ter persuage me 'g'inst
doin' sich a 'stracted t'ing. But 'twa'n't
no use; dat nigger's Ebil Eye had tuck
ontire 'session er me, en de debil hisse'f
couldn' er stopped my gwine home wid
'im on de fif' Sunday, lak he said I
mus'.
"Twuz de Ebil Eye, nuttin' but de
Ebil Eye. Yas'm, dat tuckey gobbler nig-
ger had one er dem same kin' er eyes dat
sneks en cats has; blinks en balls en
blars en wallin's en all; en de enfloons
er dem gray-green eyes er his'n seeged
