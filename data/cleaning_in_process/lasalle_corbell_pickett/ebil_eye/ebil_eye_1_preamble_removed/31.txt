I kin rackerlack how I use ter run ebby

drink de res' down wid a gulp, en smack

tas'e. Den he'd say, 'Yer, li'l' gal!' en
han' me de dreenin's dat wuz lef' in de
bottom er de glass fer me ter groan ober
de goodness er; en I'd groan ober hit,
