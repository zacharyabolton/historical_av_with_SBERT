EBIL EYE
Lawd en his party had all de enfloons,
dey had ontire 'session er de whole
premuses atter de Ole Man's fall. Dey
owned Heaben en ebbyt'ing dar wuz up
dar-en you know, my brederin, hit's
all gole up dar in Heaben; no silber-
not a single piece er silber. (Dar neb-
ber wuz but thutty pieces er silber in
de whole place, en ole Judas 'Skariot
buried dem thutty pieces in de potter's
fiel', en ebby single piece er hit tuck
root, en growed, en growed, en growed,
en dat's de 'kazhun, my brederin, er all
dis trouble yer now-dat potter's fiel'
silber.)
"De houses en de streets en de styar
steps en de breas'-plates, en eben down

dar in Heaben. Dey owned all de banks,
