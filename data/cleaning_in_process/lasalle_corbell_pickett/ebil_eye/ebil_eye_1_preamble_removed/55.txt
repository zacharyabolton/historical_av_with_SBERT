EBIL EYE
49
"De Senator's clark writ up a paper,
en meked granny put her X mark 'pon
hit; den he went 'long in de office en
git a law-tony ter byar witness dat de
mark wuz all hern. Dey call dat paper
some kin' er contrackshun, en dey
meked out one er dem same contrack-
shuns ebby yeah 'twell I wuz free, en
could mek one er my own.
Po' granny
tuck down wid de rheumatiz, howsom-
ebber, en 'ceasted dis life 'bout a munf
fo' de time come fer me ter mek my
firs' barg'in fer myse'f.
"All er de days endurin' de time atter
dat time, dough, wuz Sunday ter me.
I could skasely b'liebe dat dat po', piti-
ful me w'at use ter be me wuz de
same sho-nuff me dat I wuz now. I
had ebbyt'ing in de wide worl' dat I
wanted. I went ter my own chutch
