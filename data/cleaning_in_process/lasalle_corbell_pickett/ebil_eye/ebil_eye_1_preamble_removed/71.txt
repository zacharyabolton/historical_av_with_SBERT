LI'L BLUE LIGHT
WESTMORELAND County, the "Ath-
ens of Virginia," extended in 1653 from
the Machoactoke River to the falls of
the Pawtomake above the Nacostin
Towne. Some of the most renowned
men were born within its borders-
Washington, Richard Henry Lee, and
his three brothers, Thomas, Francis,
and Arthur; General Henry Lee, Mon-
roe, and Judge Bushrod Washington,
and many others.
