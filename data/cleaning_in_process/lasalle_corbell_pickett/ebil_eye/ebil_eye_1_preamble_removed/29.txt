EBIL EYE
23
"Well, w'en you has clum ez many
stiles ez I has, you'll fin' out dat ebby
fence, w'edder hit's a red fence er w'ed-
der hit's a brack fence, er w'atsomebber
kin' er fence 'tis, has got two sides ter
hit, en 'pear lak bofe pyars er Tommys
hain't nebber on de same side er de fence
at de same time. Ef dey wuz dey'd see
dat mos' ebby Tommy is got a tar'pin
cotchin' on ter en hangin' holt er him.
"Hit's proned inter me ter look happy,
marm, en I allers meks my bes' en-
deabor ter keep my dorgs at de back
gate, fer ef I's 'bleeged ter eat dirt, I
hain't gwine ter let folks tromple ober
hit 'fo' I eats hit. I's gwine ter try en
eat hit clean ef I kin. But I'll tell you
a secret, en you kin keep hit in yo' own
secret 'session, en dat is dis: I hain't
nebber seed a real, sho-nuff happy day
