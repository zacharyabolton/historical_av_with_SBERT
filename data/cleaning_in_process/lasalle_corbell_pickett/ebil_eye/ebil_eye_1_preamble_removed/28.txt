EBIL EYE
Tommy, didn' know nuttin' 'tall 'bout
de tar'pin habin' de tom-cat's tail in his
mouf. De boy Tommy wuz on dis side
er de fence, en all he saw wuz de tom-
cat's haid ober de fence lookin' down at
de fat mouse, wid de tears a trickerlin'
down his face. De boy, de real Tommy,
couldn' see bofe sides er de fence endur-
in' de same time, en dat's de reason he
had sich a contempshus un'erstan'in' er
de tom-cat's way er doin' bizness-sot-
tin' dar, lak he wuz paddelized 'pon top
er de fence a weepin', 'stead er jumpin'
down en wukin' fer de wittles dat wuz
jes' 'kumerlatin' hitse'f ret 'fo' his eyes.
'Twuz bad 'nuff on de tom-cat, ez 'twuz,
widout habin' de real Tommy flingin'
rocks at 'im, fer de po' tom-cat couldn'
jump for'ards, en ez de case stood, he
didn' want ter jump back'ards.
