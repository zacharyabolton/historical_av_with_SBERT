'Suiting the action to the word, she
squeezed the supply pipe, putting out
the light. With a face full of terror,
as the odor of the gas filled the room,
she rushed to meet me, blubbering,
'I's gwine ter pack my chiss, en go
stret back ter Wes'mo'lan'. I b'liebed
fum de firs' dat dis wuz ole Satan's wuk
en now dat I's done smelt his brimstone
en sulphur I knows hit. En mo' dan
dat, I knowed fum de ve'y firs' dat I
didn' hab no bizness ter be projeckin'
'long no sich ongodlishness, but ef I'sfer-
gibben dis time I'll stick ter de fiah dat
bu'ns, en I'll nebber fool wid no mo'
li'l blue lights. I laks de city, but de
proudness don't count w'en yo' haid is
done en laid low. Nar, suh! Dat hit
don't, en I hain't a gwine ter splunge
