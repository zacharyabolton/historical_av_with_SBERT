Some folks ain't nebber gwine ter hab
no eyes, en Jemimy's one er dem folks.
"Miss Libbie, jes' ez Ispuk dem wuds
ter Jemimy I kotch de Campbell twin-
kle in de chile's eyes, en seed de Mayo
faber, too. Hit struck me all in a heap,
lak a ole ax on a hick'ry chunk, dat dis
wuz yo' chile; was my Miss Libbie
Tyler's gran'chile, wid all her'n en his
ma's li'l' ole-time tricks in 'im.
"D'reckly I kotch de Campbell en de
Mayo faber in de chile I stunt ret up en
drapped 'im a cutchy 'cordin' ter de
quality er his blood. Den I tole 'im
howdy en axt him how he sergashuate
hisse'f, en axt 'im atter all de fambly en
all er de kunnexions, den I wiped de
cheer off wid my ap'un en axt 'im ter
tek a seat en set down.
