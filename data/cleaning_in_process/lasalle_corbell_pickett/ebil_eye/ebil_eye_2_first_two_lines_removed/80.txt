"Jes' ez de chile spuk out lak dat
'bout my ole Harry hit distracted de
intention er dat audacious Jemimy,
who wuz at de i'nin' boa'd i'nin' de
w'ite folkses shuts, en she up en say,
"Granny, ain't dat Miss Simpkins's
chile?" "You nigger," says I, "hain't
you nebber gwine ter l'arn a grain er
sense? Atter all my raisin' er you, you
kyan' tell yit a gemman's son, bawn en
bred en biskit-fed, fum po' w'ite trash?
Hain't you nebber gwine ter know blood
w'en you see's hit? Dat chile's got blood,
en dem Simpkinses ain't got a single
drap, fer dar gran'pa wa'n't nuttin' but
a cap'in er de paterrollers, en dar pa's
pa wuz a nigger-trader, en nigger-traders
en paterrollers is de lowes' bizness in
de wide worl'. Dis yer chile's blood is
ez blue ez endego en ez w'ite ez wax.
