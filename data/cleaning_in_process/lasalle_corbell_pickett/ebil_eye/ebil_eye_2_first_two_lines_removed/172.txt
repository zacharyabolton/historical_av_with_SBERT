"Dar warn't nar' 'ceitful bone in my
ole ooman's breas', needer, en she wuz
'spectful ter ebbybody, en you nebber
yeard tell er her gibin' nobody no sass'-
ness, ner slingin' er her jaw at ole folks,
ner year er her flippin' her petty-coats,
ner trapesin' 'roun', ner spyin', ner put-
tin' her mouf in yudder folks's bizness,
ner flingin' her time away gossickin', ner
bein' coupled up 'long no low down
lazy trash (dem kin' dat 'pariently fat-
tens on a'r). En she nebber sample
nuttin' dat didn' 'b'long ter her, needer.
Anudder t'ing sartin en sho', Marse
Da', 'bout my ole ooman, Kalline-
she nebber tole a lie; nar, sur, she'd
shame de debil ebby time. En ef de
bressed Lawd spyars me en I kin git
money nuff, I'se gwine ter put a nice,
