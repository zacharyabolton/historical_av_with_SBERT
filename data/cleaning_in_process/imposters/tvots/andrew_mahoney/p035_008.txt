Respectfully returned to Capt R. S. Lacey Sub Asst Comm with the information that James Harris is residing at Jenning's Ordinary about 40 miles from Richmond on the Danville and Richmond Rail Road and is employed there by the Rail Road Company I am informed that Harris has two other wifes one at his present location and the other at Richmond I would also State that the within mentioned papers in relation to Harris are returned to Capt. Lacey with endorsement on the 9th of January 1868.
