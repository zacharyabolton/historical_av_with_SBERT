We start tomorrow for the Rappahanock & as I dont when I shall have a chance to write to you again I thought I would write you a few lines this evening. Mr Maury had church this morning in camp. he had a very slim congregation, but he gave ous a very nice sermon. Give my love to Ash & Mike when you write to them tell them I hope to see them before long. Tell Pa that Cousin Ash said he would speak to him about my going to Lexington to school. Give my love to sister Seal & tell her I was sory I did get to see her when I was in town the other day excuse this short not as I have nothing else to say enclosed  will find a note for cousin will you
