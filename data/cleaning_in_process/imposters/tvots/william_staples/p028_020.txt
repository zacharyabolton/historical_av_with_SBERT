A colored woman named Izetta (formerly belonged to Mays near here) is living on my Farm and requests me to write to the authorities at Staunton and get them to make her son Jack aged 15 years and who has been at Staunton for two or 3 years come home to her at once and to collect his hire and remit it to her to my care person, had no right to employ him -- without her permission said Boys Father is not living She seems not to be able to control her Boys of whom she has several
