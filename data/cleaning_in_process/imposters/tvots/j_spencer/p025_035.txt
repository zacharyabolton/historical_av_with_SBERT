Saml O. arrived yesterday He Bot Shoes from B Pallerson & Bosk, No. 6 Bellween Charles & Hanover. he refers us to that house as the best in Bath. Davis waited on him, he also promise Daniel Miller & co to get us to Examine there stock. Someone waited on him He is alone with [unclear: Destries] & co The Piece of goods I wrote you about his Buying at 1.30 he Bot from Bevan & Co. He Bot good coffee for 25 cents From what he told me I think he has Bot a good lot of Dry goods. He is goods will come to hand tomorrow. I write this by request of Nelson as he promise those parties to get us to call on them. If you can have the Dry goods in Staunton friday write us by Thursdays Mail
