I was truly glad to here from You. You Must pardon Me for delaying so long. I would of answered Sooner but My time is so Much taken up that I put it of from time to time untill four weeks has Elapsed Since your Much Esteemed [unclear: letter] come to hand. You Speak Well for Staunton She is far ahead of our town in Schools I dont think there is a first class school in Our town at this time but I suppose Some Gentlemen With literary taste will establish One-- You also pay quite a compliment to the people of Winchester in Speaking of their Business qualifications I think My Self business is conducted differant in Winchester from what it is in Staunton but whethe it is conducted to a greater advantage I cant tell-- as the people Seems to prosper in business up there as well as Winchester. I am sorry to inform you that Mother has been very sick I really thought she would not recover but she is getting better. I hope she will recover her health again. Tif arrived from Moorefield on yesterday she is well but is talking of going back again in a couple of weeks All the family are well you Must write soon again. Love to all
