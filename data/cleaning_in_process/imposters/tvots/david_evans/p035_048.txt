Home again I rived in good time for Brother was looking for me he ask Magnus when I was coming back he thought I was going to Stay three or four days I would have Stayed if he hadnt of had Business to attend to at Court The next time I come I want to Stay from Saturday untill Thursday for it will be the onley chance I will have They will open the Factory next month & I will be Keep closely confined. I will have to goand return the Same day. I hope I can get of Saturday week to Stay three or four days at your home I know I would Enjoy my Self Magnus sayes he is going two we could have a nice time Fishing. Ella wants to go two I want Magnus to take her She Says he will think a bout it.
