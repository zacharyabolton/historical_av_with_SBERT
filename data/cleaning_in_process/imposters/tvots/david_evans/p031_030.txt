Ella had a good warm Supper prepard for us which Magnus and my self did ample justice too you may look for Magnus down after you in too weeks if not sooner Ella says she dont think she will let you stay that long so you will haft to hurry up & get through with your spining this week so you can come up with him next week I think I willgo over to night and tell Ella to send for you this week for we cant do with out you I have thought of you all day and wonderd if Miss Mollie thought of DH Evans whilest she was Spining. I would like to see you at the Spinning Wheel I know you look interesting. you must Excuse this short note I have been keep so Bussy since I came home that I have not had time to write I am interrupted ever few minuts while I am writing this Excuse all mistakes and write to me soon My love to your Ma, & all of my friends I send a kiss to Miss Mollie all are well
