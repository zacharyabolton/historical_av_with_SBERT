You did not Say in your dear letter wether you would go to the Springs or not I hope you will go with me my love for I am compeld to go my self and I want you to go with me. I will be down a bout Seven O clock if I live. I think you had better make up your mind to go to the Springs you may have forgotten to menchen it in your letter. I thought as you did not menchen it that you declined decline going. Dr Arthur will come with me Tomorrow. Good bye my love untill we meet tomorrow
