Your dear & welcome letter of the 19th has bin recd & read with much pleasure, you Must blame me this for writing you a long letter. I have bin so Bussey all the Morning that I have not had time to write to you my love. I am so glad that Ada has come so you can spend the Summer with Ella she will moove next week in her new house I am sorry I cant pay you my usual visit this Saturday. Brother is mooveing & it will be imposibe for me to get off I will be down Saturday a week for Surtien I still love you the Same with all my heart & Soul. I must Close Else I wont get this note in the office in time for the Mail My love to your Ma & Pa Excuse this writing it was dun in haste Ella & family are all well I send love & a Kiss to you my love. I do wish I could come down this Saturday to See you but I cant God Bless you good bye
