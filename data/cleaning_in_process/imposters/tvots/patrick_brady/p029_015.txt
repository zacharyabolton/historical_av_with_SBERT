Yours of the 13this to hand, am Sorry You had the trouble to copy the agreement, what I wanted was a copy of the paper I left with you in pencil, extracts taken from the Ledger showing the alterations made therein; which I should like to have at your earliest convenience. I think upon reflection that our best course will be to put him Bankruptcy My council here Mr. Bullett will add a postscript to my letter and I beg you will consult with him in the matter.
