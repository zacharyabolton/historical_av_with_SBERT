We came here yesterday after a little skirmish with the en-enemy at [unclear: Auoborn] in which we had one man wounded, & we killed & wounded several of the enemy & routed them & followed here where we had quite a fight with them, [unclear: Hiles] Corps only being engaged -- he had a good many killed & wounded and lost 5 pieces of artillery & a few prisoners our corps advanced on the right but was not much engaged & we only had 5 men wounded -- none of our county people were engaged --  We are getting along very well -- the enemy retreated last night beyond Manassa where our advance went today -- I do not know what we will do next -- The courier goes soon & I can only say I am well & getting along very well -- Good by & God bless you -- My love to all
