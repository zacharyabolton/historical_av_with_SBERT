I wrote you a few days ago about the lost box of books and rec'd no answer The Box is marked and directed as follows--as per Invoice. NY 13 February 1869 Shipped per Steamer C C Knight Gov't Transportation. Marked U S John Scott Staunton Va Care Geo Q White Mayor  Richmond Va. No 3180. 1 Box Rev RM Manly Sup Schls Richmond informs me that such St'r does not sail to Richmond. Thinks it goes to Alexandria. We are much pressed for books
