With many thanks for your kind favors I enclose you ten dollars for my wife Martha ann please send her on to Chambersburg without delay I can meet here there almost anytime as I only live sixteen miles from there If you write to me amedeately on receipt of this I ought to receive the letter in four or five days Please to let me know in your letter when she will start that I may know when to be there to meet her or send her that she may arrive in Chambersburg on the 11th of Dec. and I will be there to meet her. Direct your letter as usual to Loudon Franklin Co. Pa. care of Mr. Eli McCullock. Please except my thanks for your kind favors
